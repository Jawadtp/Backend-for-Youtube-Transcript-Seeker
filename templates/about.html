Yoogle is a tool with which youtube video transcripts can be searched. Using the link of the video and the search-term, the video's transcript is searched for the search-term. All matches are displayed with their respective timestamps which navigates to the video at the corresponding timestamp.

Yoogle can search through playlists too. Simply paste the link of the video/playlist you want to search and enter the search term. Click submit and let Yoogle do the magic.

Currently Yoogle supports searching for English transcripts only. Help us expand by contributing to the project.
